Summary

An eight-week project for school were we made a game for mobile and set in for free in the Google play store.
The subject for our game was Mythe, so we chose the rainbow serpent (a fable animal) to be the theme of our game.
This is my first mobile game I've worked so far and with the biggest team so far (10 people). And I am proud of the results!

Game features:

-Endless runner
-Online highscore
-Static and moving obstacles
-Increasing difficulty
-Randomized chunks
-Tutorial chunks for beginners
-Snake movement
-Particles for game feel
