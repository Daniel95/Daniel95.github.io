Summary

An eight-week project for school were we made a game for mobile
and published it in the Google Play store.

The subject of our game was had to be a mythe, so we chose the
rainbow serpent (a fable animal) to be the theme of our game.
This is my first mobile game I've worked so far and with the
biggest team so far (10 people).

And I am proud of the results!

Game features:

-Endless runner
-Online highscore
-Static and moving obstacles
-Increasing difficulty
-Randomized chunks
-Tutorial chunks for beginners
-Snake movement
-Particles for game feel
