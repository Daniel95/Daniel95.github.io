Summary

Winter is coming is our first game that we worked on together with game artists.
It also was the last project of the first year of our study at Mediacollege Amstedam.
The goal of the game is to survive as long as possible. To survive you have to kill
enemies with your bow, and dodge their attacks.

You can aim your bow by holding down the left mouse button to shoot more accurate,
or you can spam it to shoot loads of arrows fast but with less accuracy.

Game features:

-Upgrade your character
-Aiming your bow
-Waves with enemies, and eventually a boss wave
