Summary

This is supposed to be a a mix of a puzzle game and an action endless runner game.
My goal is to bring it out on mobile once its fully fleshed out.

You movement is automatic.
You can adjust your path by click the left mouse button. This will shoot out
a grappling hook that you can use sling around the level.
This is game is in an early phase of development.
expect bugs.

Game features:

-Grappling Hook
-Automatic Movement
-Procedurally generated Level (coming soon!)
