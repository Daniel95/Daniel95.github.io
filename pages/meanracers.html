Summary

Meanracers is a project that really learned me a lot. I had previously
thought of the idea of a multiplayer platformer game, and when our school gave us
the assignment make our own vacancy for a game that other students could apply on,
this was the perfect opportunity to try and make that game.

We had one student who knew a lot about JavaScript, so we decided that in
order to learn more about it we are going to make this game in JavaScript.

The goal of the game is to go up as fast as possible by jumping.
You automatically move left or right when you walk against a wall
or another player. When one player remains he wins the game and the game restarts.
It is fast, simple and exciting to play!

Game features:

-Online Multiplayer
-Endless Level made with my Chunk Editor
-Easy to play!
-Mobile opportunities
