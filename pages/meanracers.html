Summary

Meanracers is a project that really learned me alot. I had previously thought of the idea of a multiplayer platformer game,
and when our school game us the assignment make our own vacancy for a game that other students could apply on,
this was the perfect opportunity to try and make that game.
We had one student who knew alot about Javascript,
so we decided in order to learn more about it we are going to make this game in Javascript.

The goal of the game is to go up as fast as possible by jumping. You automaticly move left or right when you walk against a wall or another player. When one player remains he wins the game and the game restarts.
It is fast, simple and exciting to play!

Game features:

-Online Multiplayer
-Endless Level made with my Chunk Editor
-Easy to play!
-Mobile opportunities
