Summary

I started this project in the summer vacation. It started with a
simple idea of a cube that you steer through a level
and if you hit a wall you have try again in another level of the same difficulty.

When school began we chose different smaller project that we worked on to complete.
I chose the following projects: Perlin Noise Terrain,
Destructable Terrain, Water Shader and Terrain Shader.
Each time we had one week to complete the project and another week to
reflect on what we wanted to do later on.
During this time i merged all projects into one and made a it into a game.

The result is a cube that you can steer through an endless terrain, with
different biomes like land and islands.
These are connected to eachother with paths that you can navigate over.
There's moving water and land with different colors, made with shaders.
The player leaves a trail of destruction behind that devastates the land so
you can't retrace your steps.

Game features:

-Infinite Terrain
-Paths that connect land and islands
-Shaders like water and colored terrain
-Editable Terrain
