Summary

A Tower Defense game where you have to defend the treehouse by placing towers,
upgrading them with resources, and buy and defend resource spots to earn more resources!
The rounds are made with a dynamic points system, every round the computer has so many points to spend on units,
each unit costs a certain amount of points depending on their strength.
Each round the points the computer has to spend increases and so the round gets harder and harder in an endless wave system.

Game features:

-Big map that you can move through
-Defend resource spots for more money
-Place and upgrade towers 3 different towers
-dynamic wave system that spends points
