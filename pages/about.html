About Me
My name is Daniel Brand, I live in Alphen aan den Rijn, the Netherlands.
I am a junior Game Developer, currently studying at Mediacollege Amsterdam.

I am looking for an internship at a 2D game studio, I have the most experience
with 2D games, mostly on mobile.
On most projects I work on I am the lead developer. This doesn't mean I decide

and knew what we could and could not do in the time we had.

In my free time I usually work on spare time projects or continue school projects
and play games.
Games that I really enjoy are PC games like Darksouls, Civilization, World of Warcraft,
Battlefield, Brawhalla.
I also enjoy to play old RTS games with friends such as Age of Empires and Rise of Nations.
