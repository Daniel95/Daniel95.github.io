Summary

For this project, students from all kinds of studies were selected to
participate in this excelention project.
We would work on this project all week long, from 10 till 5 and we would
also no longer participate in normal class for 4 weeks.

The goal of this project was to make an eyecatcher for visitors of
MediaBytes 2016 in Mediacafe in Vondelpark Amsterdam.
The end result was a big projection in the middle of the central hall where we
visualized the connections made in the Connectif app.
This app was made to connect students and professionals with eachother by swiping
eachother left or right, kind of like tinder.

Features:

-Users can interact with the projection by connecting with eachother in the connectif app.
